How to Get Started

  1. Find a trained coach and a certified pit setup.
  2. Build sprint form and takeoff rhythm before full vaults.
  3. Use short-approach drills for safer technical progression.
  4. Track approach marks, consistency, and weekly progress.

About Pole Vault

Pole vault combines speed, timing, and technical precision. Improvement comes from consistent plant mechanics, controlled takeoff, and smart progression.

This site is designed as a simple starting guide for beginners who want structure before chasing bigger heights.
